Connect groups

We believe real relationships and life happens in the context of community.

Our Connect Groups are held every other week on Wednesday and Thursdays in different homes and venues, in an area near you.

Connect groups create opportunities for us to get to know people with similar interests, while also growing in our faith.

If you have any questions about joining a connect group please contact home@hischurchlondon.co.uk.

Safety

Your child’s safety is our first priority. All our His Kids volunteers pass a background check. We use a security tag systm for child check-in and pick-up. And a paging system for parents/guardians in case we need to reach you during the service.

Youth

ROOTS is geared for anyone in high school. We want to see you grow in your faith, your character and your relationships. You belong here and we believe God has a magnificent plan and purpose for you and we want to help you discover it and live it.

We encourage the youth of His Church London to be involved in everything we do from inviting, participating and serving on a team.
