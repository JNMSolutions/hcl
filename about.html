Our Vision is

COME | GROW | LEAD

Come

Our aim at His Church London is to create an environment where people know they can COME, bring their friends and experience Jesus for themselves.

Grow

God has an incredible plan for your life!

As you discover more about who God is and who He created you to be, you will find purpose and freedom.

To help you discover Gods plan and purpose for you, we have a course that runs over 4 weeks called GROWTH TRACK.

Come and join us on our next course.

Lead

We believe that your life does make a difference and that you are called to influence in the environments God has placed you in.

Leadership is not about titles or positions, its about discovering your gifts and using them to make a difference in others lives. There are many opportunities available for you to use your gift to impact the people around you.

Our values:

Love | Unity | Faith | Excellence | Missional | Celebration | Generosity

Small Groups

We believe that life happens in the context of relationships and community. The purpose of our small group meetings is to bring people together.

Small Groups provide a place for us to connect with each other, a place where we can find support and encouragement and a place to grow together.

Come and Join us

Our Groups meet in different locations every other week. There is always a place for you!
